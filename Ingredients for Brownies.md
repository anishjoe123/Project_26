| ----------------- | ------------ |
| Melted butter     | 1 cup        |

| Cocoa Powder      | 1/2 cup      |

| Eggs              | 4            |
| All-purpose Flour | 1 1/2 cups   |
| Baking Powder     | 1/2 teaspoon |
| Salt              | 1/2 teaspoon |
| Walnut Halves     | 1/2 cup      |
